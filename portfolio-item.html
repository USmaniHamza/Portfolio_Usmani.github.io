BRACU Classroom

An online class management system similar to that of Google Classroom but it has a platform of it's own with it's own student performance tracking system purely based on Data Visualization for both students and faculty members for better communication . The tools that aided this project are JAVA SE-8 , basic Java-Swing and JFreeCharts library .

This image is based on the UI of the teachers home page after logging in, the table platform as we see is to mark out students from all section under the responsibility of the teachers, the students that need attention is going to be auto-marked out based on the weak performances as soon as they receive their scores . An option of reviewing overall student performances based on the first 50 overall marks before the mid-term exams so that students can get a heads up before the finals in order to improve their performances .

The fields at the bottom of the page is to filter out the students in order to submit their scores which is handled by a database at the back-end .




This image indicates where the particular students were lacking in performance compared to what the students were supposed to achieve in order to receive good scores .

The Blue Shade indicates what was expected as output performances from the students and the Red Shade indicates what the students in particular actually achieved as scores .




This image is about Data Visualization of the scores students achieved within the first fifty marks using Spider Charts or Radar Charts .

The edges close to the arc of the circle will determine how well the student is performing throughout the mid-terms. A small message-box telling the user what each degrees mean for example the edge at 45 degrees mean what the student achieved in the best Quiz marks taken out of the total Quizzes given whereas the edge at 180 degrees show what the student achieved in the assignments etc .

If the students fall under a certain zone or if the delta symbol at the heart of the Spider Chart, shrinks down to certain level then the prediction of identifying weak students would work in order to mark the students as a reminder to improve their performances .

This overlaid bar chart helps to visualize the overall performances of all the students (section-wise) under the faculty's responsibility. The red bar represents the total marks of 50 whereas the blue bar represents the overall Mean of the scores achieved by the students in the particular section .

This image shows us the data of the overall scores the students were successful in achieving before finals since the marks that is in comparison is out total 100 . The Red bar being the total scores and the Blue bar being the scores actually achieved

Together with the help of Data Visualization techniques for both the students and the teachers, a better means of communication can be achieved which will help both the student and their teachers to follow-up throughout the semester .
