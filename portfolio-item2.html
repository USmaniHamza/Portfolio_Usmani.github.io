Mateam Lubnan

Mateam Lubnan is an idea of a joint-venture Lebanese bistro with bringing the most scrumptious Lebanese cuisines. A webapp made with jquery and Bootstrap4 framework .

The header segment of the landing Index Page contains a nav-bar with Pop-up modals (Login,Researving a table). A Carousel is added using Javascript to show the latest news and ads regarding the restaurant where the user has the freedom to pause and play the slide as an advantage to web accessibility. A clear way to navigate to all the pages has been set in the navigation pane in the header segment at the top .

Lastly the logo has been made using FreeLogoDesign.org which summarizes the type of brand it is with the appropriate colours and style .




The mid segment of the Index Page page consists of clearly spaces panes with little details of what the restaurant chain offers aided with Bootstrap4's badges and badge-pill features to highlight the ads and make them catchy to the eye so that users pay more attention .




For the footer of the Index Page page we have a way to navigate to other pages just like in the header sections. The brief contact and address info is delivered with appropriate linkups using the fa-icons. Lastly appropriate links to the social media is provided using the appropriate icons .

For the Contact us Page we have the same layout as the index page for both the header and the footer. We have a brief information of the laid out regarding the addres together with the location of the restaurant in Google Map in the small windown pane right beside the address information. Below we have an option to reach out for the restaurant using lanline option or through skype or email .

On the second half of the Contact us Page we have an option to let viewers of our page to interact with our Web-app to deliver us their feedback on the services provided offline .

In the About us Page section we have start off with Breadcrumbs to keep track of the pages the viewers are in and to make navigation a bit simpler. A Card is used to display the records of past business with other clients. Together with a quote from a food critic right below the brief history of the restaurant .

Below we have details regarding Corporate Leadership in collapsible Cards format so that the it gives the viewers freedom choose the parts that they want to see .

Another Bootstrap4's Card feature at the bottom displaying the Facts & Figures regarding three years time-period .

Various features of Bootstrap4 together with Javascript and JQuery has been in order to make this site more accessible and ease of use .
