Omega Estiatorio

Omega Estiatorio is an idea of a joint-venture responsive Greek Restaurant Web-app, a single page application built solely on ReactJS, together with React-Strap and Redux for following a suitable Design Pattern for state management .

The header segment of the landing Index Page contains a nav-bar with Pop-up modals (Login) . The top of the header segment contains a clear way to navigate to all the pages has been set in the navigation pane in the header segment at the top .




The mid segment of the Index Page page consists of clearly spaced panes with little details of what the restaurant chain offers .




For the footer of the Index Page page we have a way to navigate to other pages just like in the header sections. The brief contact and address info is delivered with appropriate linkups using the fa-icons . Lastly appropriate links to the social media is provided using the appropriate icons .

The second page from the Index, we have our Menu Items page highlights the special dishes that is served in the restaurant at peak seasons. At the top of the page we have Breadcrumbs for a much easier user-understandable approach for navigation .

The Menu Items page also has a feature for curious viewers to go through the dishes to check out the reviews from past critics about the type of food it is, with a suitable rating and comments displayed right beside the dish in attention. The feature allows the viewers to add their own reviews about particular dishes . The Submit Comment allows a pop-up to appear for the viewers to throw a rating and the appropriate comments for the dish . The Breadcrumbs at the top of the page tracks the current location of the page the viewer is in .

For the Contact us Page we have the same layout as the index page for both the header and the footer. We have a brief information of the laid out regarding the addres together with the location of the restaurant in Google Map in the small windown pane right beside the address information. Below we have an option to reach out for the restaurant using hot-line option or through skype or via email .

On the second half of the Contact us Page we have an option to let viewers of our page to interact with our Web-app to deliver us their feedback on the services provided offline .

Lastly in the About us page together with the Breadcrumbs to navigate and track the current location of the viewers on our Web-app, to introduce the restaurant-business in brief with a small comment from a food-critic on how the restaurant services were to them. A Card displaying a brief-info on the turnover history with other clients of the restaurant .

We end our About us Page with the introduction of higher executives or stakeholders of the business including their designation stylized suitably .

Together with ReactJS, Redux and React-strap a wide variety of functionality has been achieved for better user experience and better accessibility .
